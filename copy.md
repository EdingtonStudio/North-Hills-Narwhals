## The pitch
- Eyebrow: The pitch
- Heading: Grown men. Real baseball. North of the rivers.
- Intro: This is a wood-and-metal-bat hardball team forming in Pittsburgh's North Hills for guys 35 and up who never quite got it out of their system. Here's the honest version.

**Card 1 — What it is — Actual baseball**
Nine innings, ninety-foot bases, a real diamond. Fun and competitive, which is code for we keep score but we're nice about it. Post-game beverages strongly encouraged.

**Card 2 — What it isn't — Not softball**
Not slow-pitch, not a tryout you can fail, not a midlife crisis (probably). If you show up and you're 35-plus, you're a Narwhal. That's the whole bar.

**Card 3 — What we need — You, mostly**
A glove, cleats that still fit, and a willingness to stretch before the game like an adult. Bring a buddy who can pitch and you're basically a co-founder.

## Why a narwhal
- Eyebrow: Why a narwhal
- Heading: The unicorn of the sea, the dad of the diamond.
- Paragraph 1: The narwhal is a real animal that looks completely made up. Rarely seen, weirdly competitive, and carrying a six-foot tusk for reasons marine biologists still argue about. We related immediately.
- Paragraph 2: It also doesn't migrate far from home, peaks later than expected, and is tougher than it looks. If that's not a 35-year-old ballplayer from the North Hills, we don't know what is. Plus every good name up here was already taken.

## FAQ
- Eyebrow: Before you ask
- Heading: The dugout FAQ

**How good do I have to be?**
Played in high school, college, or beer league since? All fine. We're aiming for fun and competitive. Range is optional, showing up is not.

**Is this actually real?**
It's real enough that there's a website. It gets very real the moment enough names hit the list. That could be you, today, instead of mowing the lawn.

**Where and when?**
North Hills fields, evenings and weekends, once we've got a roster. Think Ross, McCandless, Hampton, Shaler, West View, the whole 412-north stretch.

**What's it going to cost?**
Less than a set of irons, more than a six-pack. Real numbers once we know how many of us are in. Fields and umps aren't free, sadly.

**I'm 34.**
Close enough, we'll redshirt you. Kidding. Mostly. Sign up anyway and we'll sort it out when the season's real.

**I haven't played in 15 years.**
Perfect. So has everyone else. The first month is mostly remembering how to slide without filing a claim afterward.
